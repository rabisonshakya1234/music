about music

Music is the type of art that mixes sound and rhythm to create a usable melody line. Time, place, and cultural boundaries are all irrelevant to music. Without using any words, music can convey a mood. Additionally, it can be recorded in a written universal language that is unlike any other kind of art. The history of music is a broad subject that calls for a lot of consideration and time. An old art form that dates back to the primordial era is music. It contains a history of every human civilisation over time. Even though there are many different definitions of music, it is a cultural universal since music has been an essential part of life for all cultures throughout history. For eons, music has been transmitted orally.
